Event Production

Mercedes-Benz Launch

Orchestrating a luxury automotive brand experience from concept to execution

Role Lead Producer
Year 2023
Client Mercedes-Benz Malaysia

Project Overview

The Challenge

Mercedes-Benz required a premium showroom launch event that would reflect their brand's luxury positioning while creating an immersive experience for VIP guests and media.

The Solution

Developed a comprehensive creative strategy encompassing storyboarding, video production, motion graphics, and on-site event coordination to deliver a seamless brand experience.

My Contribution

Led the entire creative production from initial concept development through final execution, managing a cross-functional team and coordinating all visual and multimedia elements.

Key Deliverables

Storyboarding

Created detailed visual narratives for all video content, ensuring every frame aligned with Mercedes-Benz's premium brand standards.

Video Production

Directed and produced high-quality promotional videos showcasing the new showroom and vehicle lineup.

Motion Graphics

Designed dynamic animations for LED displays and digital touchpoints throughout the showroom.

On-Site Coordination

Managed live event operations, ensuring flawless execution of all multimedia elements during the launch.

Production Process

01

Concept Development

Collaborated with Mercedes-Benz marketing team to develop a creative vision that would elevate the showroom launch beyond a traditional automotive event. Focused on creating an experiential journey for guests.

02

Pre-Production

Created comprehensive storyboards, shot lists, and production schedules. Coordinated with venue management, lighting designers, and AV technicians to ensure technical feasibility.

03

Production & Post

Directed video shoots featuring the new vehicle models and showroom facilities. Produced motion graphics and edited all video content to match Mercedes-Benz's cinematic brand aesthetic.

04

Event Execution

Managed on-site operations during the launch event, coordinating real-time content playback, lighting cues, and multimedia presentations to create a seamless guest experience.
