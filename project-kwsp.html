Digital Experience

KWSP New Website

Creating an engaging launch experience for Malaysia's national pension fund digital transformation

Role Launch Gimmick Designer
Year 2024
Client KWSP (EPF Malaysia)

Project Overview

The Challenge

KWSP needed a memorable launch moment for their redesigned website that would capture attention and communicate their digital transformation to millions of Malaysian contributors.

The Solution

Designed an interactive launch animation featuring a sleek, futuristic interface that visualized the transition from legacy systems to modern digital services.

My Contribution

Conceptualized and executed the entire launch gimmick, creating motion graphics that transformed complex financial data into an engaging visual narrative.

Design Elements

Futuristic Interface

Designed a cutting-edge UI animation that showcased KWSP's commitment to digital innovation and modern service delivery.

Interactive Elements

Incorporated touch-responsive animations that allowed event attendees to explore the new website features in real-time.

Data Visualization

Transformed complex pension fund statistics into elegant, animated infographics that were both informative and visually striking.

Seamless Transitions

Created fluid motion sequences that guided viewers through the website's new features with cinematic quality.

Creative Process

01

Concept Ideation

Researched modern financial service interfaces and futuristic design trends. Developed mood boards that balanced KWSP's institutional credibility with forward-thinking innovation.

02

Storyboard & Design

Created detailed animation storyboards showing the journey from old to new. Designed custom UI elements, icons, and data visualization components that aligned with KWSP's brand guidelines.

03

Animation Production

Built the launch animation in After Effects, incorporating 3D elements and particle effects to create depth and visual interest. Fine-tuned timing and transitions for maximum impact.

04

Launch Integration

Worked with KWSP's digital team to integrate the animation into the launch event presentation. Optimized for multiple display formats including LED walls and web streaming.
