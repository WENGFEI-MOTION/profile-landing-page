Project Overview

The Challenge

Traditional advertising workflows are time-consuming and resource-intensive. Businesses needed a solution that could generate high-quality, data-driven ad creatives at scale while maintaining brand consistency.

The Solution

Marvis AI combines artificial intelligence with creative expertise to automate ad generation, optimize performance through predictive analytics, and deliver measurable results with an average CPA of RM13 per lead.

My Contribution

Led the complete branding and product design for the platform, creating an intuitive interface that makes advanced AI technology accessible to marketers of all skill levels.

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews with marketing teams to understand pain points in traditional ad creation workflows. Analyzed competitor platforms and identified opportunities for differentiation.

02

Brand Identity

Developed a modern, tech-forward visual identity that communicates innovation and reliability. Created comprehensive brand guidelines including color systems, typography, and iconography.

03

Interface Design

Designed an intuitive dashboard that simplifies complex AI operations. Focused on progressive disclosure to make advanced features accessible without overwhelming new users.

04

Testing & Iteration

Conducted multiple rounds of user testing with beta clients. Refined the interface based on feedback to optimize the creative workflow and improve conversion rates.
