Brand Strategy

Dreamory Rebranding

Building a complete brand identity from the ground up for a leading event management company

Role Brand Lead
Year 2022 - 2024
Company Dreamory Group Sdn Bhd

Project Overview

The Challenge

Dreamory Group needed a complete brand overhaul to reflect their evolution from a startup event company to a premium event management firm serving major corporate clients.

The Solution

Developed a comprehensive brand identity system including logo design, brand narrative, visual guidelines, and corporate identity materials that positioned Dreamory as industry leaders.

My Contribution

Led the entire rebranding initiative from research and strategy through design execution and implementation across all touchpoints.

Brand Elements

Logo Design

Created a modern, memorable logo that balances creativity with professionalism, incorporating symbolic elements representing dreams and memories.

Brand Story

Crafted a compelling narrative that articulated Dreamory's mission, values, and unique approach to creating unforgettable experiences.

Visual Identity

Developed a complete visual system including color palettes, typography, iconography, and graphic elements for consistent brand expression.

Corporate Identity

Designed comprehensive CI materials including business cards, letterheads, presentations, and digital templates.

Brand Strategy Process

01

Discovery & Research

Conducted stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and market research to understand Dreamory's position and aspirations. Identified key differentiators and brand opportunities.

02

Strategy Development

Defined brand positioning, personality, and messaging framework. Created a brand story that resonated with both corporate clients and the internal team.

03

Visual Identity Design

Explored multiple logo concepts before refining the chosen direction. Developed the complete visual system including color theory, typography selection, and graphic language.

04

Implementation & Guidelines

Created comprehensive brand guidelines documentation. Designed all corporate identity materials and trained the team on proper brand usage across all channels.
